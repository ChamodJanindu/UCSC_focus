# UCSC Study Tracker

A high-performance academic workspace designed for UCSC students to track modules, manage credits, and achieve deep focus.

## Features

- **Holographic Roadmap:** A visually stunning and interactive curriculum roadmap.
- **Neural Past Papers:** A branching UI to track your past paper progress.
- **Light & Dark Modes:** A beautiful UI in both light and dark themes.
- **Progress Tracking:** Track your progress through the semester.
- **Timer:** A deep work timer to help you focus.
- **Achievements:** Unlock badges as you complete your work.
- **GPA Calculator:** Calculate your GPA and plan for your target GPA.

## Getting Started

1.  Clone the repository.
2.  Open `index.html` in your browser.

## Usage

1.  Select your course, year, and semester.
2.  Track your progress on the dashboard.
3.  Use the timer to focus on your studies.
4.  Master past papers in the Past Papers section.
5.  Unlock achievements in the Hall of Fame.
